About Us

We are the SoCS SU reps team. We have created this site to help with openness and transparency of the SU process as well as having a single place to show all the things that the SU reps get up to. The aim of the course reps and school rep is to ensure that your student voice is being heard. No idea or issue is to small. If you do wish to make a post, challenge an action or make a comment in general please head over to the GitHub page and make a pull request or raise an Issue.

How the Team Works Together

Not knowing where you fit in an organization, even as a volunteer, can make you feel a little lost or even a little anxious. A good way to remove this is by having a look at the organisation chart. Lincoln SU don't have a formal organisation chart (we have asked for this) that shows all College, School and Course reps - So we made one.

From this digram you can see how many 'hops' it takes to get to the SU VP of Education and where and how everyone interacts with one another. We have also added in some details about how often we meet with the SoCS leadership team so you can instantly see what meetings we go to on your behalf. None of these meetings are secret, you can ask anything about them and we will be happy to give as much detail as possible. The School and Course reps attend these meetings on your behalf so make sure you use them by making your concerns and ideas known. No idea or concern is to small.
