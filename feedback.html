SoCS FEEDBACK

We Need Your Feedback

One of the most important ways SoCS identifies areas of improvement is by getting open and honest feedback from its student population. We (the SoCS reps) need YOU to constantly tell us when things are not going well, you see areas of improvement, you have concerns and you think we should know and also if you have any general ideas of how to improve things. No idea or concern is to small!
The Google Forms facility is a shared service that all school reps have access to. This means that all responses can be read by any school rep or anyone with a '@lincolnsu.com' email. To get your own questions asked on the feedback form please raise an Issue on our GitHub repo (link at bottom of page) or send us an Email.
NOTE: All feedback is anonymous. No personal information is wanted or needed to provide feedback.
