SoCS REP FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

The course reps get asked many questions and some of them require the same support. This can include things such as sign posting, inquiring on help or support or even ways to give feedback about a lecture. To help with this we have put together this FAQs. If you want to add an FAQ then please email us or raise an issue on our GitHub.
